Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Landlords' Rights UK ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you visit landlordrights.co.uk.

Who we are

Landlords' Rights UK is an independent website providing guidance on UK landlord legislation. For privacy enquiries, contact us at: hello@landlordrights.co.uk.

What data we collect

We do not require you to create an account or provide personal information to use this site. However, the following data may be collected:

  • Cookies & local storage: We store your compliance checklist progress in your browser's local storage. This data never leaves your device.
  • Analytics: We may use Google Analytics to understand how the site is used (pages visited, time on site, device type). This is anonymised and subject to Google's privacy policy.
  • Advertising: We use Google AdSense to display adverts. Google may use cookies to personalise ads based on your browsing history. You can opt out via Google Ad Settings.

Affiliate links

Some links on this site are affiliate links. If you click through and make a purchase, we may receive a commission. We do not share any personal data with affiliate partners beyond standard referral tracking (e.g. a cookie that records you clicked from our site). We always disclose affiliate relationships on the page.

Third-party services

  • Google Analytics — usage analytics. Google Privacy Policy.
  • Google AdSense — advertising. Subject to Google's advertising policies.
  • Vercel — our hosting provider. May log server-side request data (IP address, browser, request time) for security and performance. Vercel Privacy Policy.

Your rights under UK GDPR

As a UK resident you have the right to: access data held about you; request correction or deletion; object to processing; and lodge a complaint with the ICO at ico.org.uk.

Because we collect minimal personal data and store checklist data only in your own browser, most of these rights have limited applicability. For any enquiry, email hello@landlordrights.co.uk.

Cookies

We use the following cookies and storage mechanisms:

  • cookie-ok (local storage) — records that you dismissed the cookie notice.
  • chk-* (local storage) — stores your compliance checklist progress. Never transmitted to our servers.
  • Google Analytics — _ga, _gid cookies for anonymised analytics.
  • Google AdSense — advertising cookies (where consent is given).

You can clear local storage at any time via your browser settings. To opt out of Google Analytics, use the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the site evolves. Material changes will be noted with an updated date at the top of this page.
